Resistor - Fixed Resistors

电阻类型 Types of Resistors

固定电阻 Fixed Resistors

线绕电阻 Wirewound Resistor

结构图 Structural Diagram:

工艺 Manufacturing Process：

  • 把电阻丝（镍铬、锰铜等）绕在⼀个绝缘骨架架（陶瓷等）上制成，电阻丝的：材料、长短、粗细，决定着阻值的大小，最后在外层涂上绝缘材料（环氧树脂、漆皮等）进⾏密封。

    The resistive wire (typically made of nickel-chromium or manganin) is wound around an insulating core (such as ceramic). The resistance value is determined by the material, length, and thickness of the resistive wire. Finally, the outer layer is coated with an insulating material (such as epoxy resin or enamel) for sealing and protection.

特点 Features：

  • 精度较高：最高可达±0.01%。

    High precision: Accuracy can reach up to ±0.01%.

  • 功率较高：可以承受较大功率，最高可以有几kw左右。

    High power rating: Capable of handling large power loads, up to several kilowatts.

  • 成本略高：100个100Ω，精度+-1%，功率13.5W的价格，约为754元。

    Relatively high cost: For example, a batch of 100 units of 100 Ω resistors with ±1% tolerance and 13.5 W power rating costs around 754 CNY.

  • 温度系数较低：20ppm/℃~200ppm/℃。

    Low temperature coefficient: Typically 20 ppm/℃ to 200 ppm/℃.

  • 特别注意：存在寄生电容与寄生电感，不适合高频场景（高频场景有WIFI，蓝牙，卫星通信，雷达系统等）

    Important note: Wirewound resistors exhibit parasitic capacitance and parasitic inductance, making them unsuitable for high-frequency applications (such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, satellite communications, and radar systems).

适用场景 Applicable Scenarios:

  • 常用于：过载保护、高功率设备中，例如：⼤型机床电路中。

    Commonly used for overload protection and high-power equipment, such as in the circuits of large industrial machines.

碳膜电阻 Carbon Film Resistor

结构图 Structural Diagram:

工艺 Manufacturing Process:

  • 把⾼温下分解出来的结晶碳，蒸馏沉积在陶瓷棒上，随后在碳膜上刻出螺旋槽，通过螺旋槽的宽度和⻓度来控制阻值，最后在外层图上环氧树脂来密封。

    The crystalline carbon, obtained through high-temperature decomposition, is distilled and deposited onto a ceramic rod. A helical groove is then engraved on the carbon film, with the groove’s width and length determining the resistance value. Finally, the outer layer is coated with epoxy resin for sealing.

特点 Features：

  • 精度略低：常用±5%，最高±1%。

    Lower precision: Commonly ±5%, with a maximum of ±1%.

  • 功率范围：1/8W ~ 5W 之间

    Power rating: Typically lower than wirewound resistors, suitable for standard electronic circuits.

  • **成本较低：**100个1kΩ，精度±5%，功率1/4W的价格，约为4.4元

    Lower cost: For example, a batch of 100 units of 1 kΩ resistors with ±5% tolerance and 1/4 W power rating costs approximately 4.4 CNY.

  • 温度系数一般：大致是200ppm/℃~500ppm/℃

    Moderate temperature coefficient: Typically around 200 ppm/℃ to 500 ppm/℃.

  • **负温度系数：**随着温度升高，阻值减小

    Negative temperature coefficient (NTC): The resistance decreases as the temperature rises.

适用场景 Applicable Scenarios:

  • 对精度要求不高，且要控制成本的设备，例如：电动玩具、老式电器。

    Used in devices where high precision is not required and cost control is important, such as electric toys and older household appliances.

金属膜电阻 Metal Film Resistor

结构图 Structural Diagram:

工艺 Manufacturing Process:

  • 在高温真空环境下，将金属膜（如镍铬合金）镀在陶瓷棒上，随后通过刻蚀的工艺 ，在金属膜上刻出螺旋槽，同样是通过螺旋槽的宽度和长度来控制阻值。

    In a high-temperature vacuum environment, a metal film (such as a nickel-chromium alloy) is deposited onto a ceramic rod. The resistance value is then controlled by etching a helical groove into the metal film, with the groove’s width and length determining the final resistance.

特点 Features：

  • 性能稳定 Stable performance

  • **精度较高：**常用±1%，最高 ±0.01%。

    High precision: Commonly ±1%, with a maximum of ±0.01%.

  • 功率范围广：50mW ~ 20W之间。

    Wide power range: From 50 mW to 20 W.

  • 成本略低：100个1KΩ，精度±1%，功率1/4W的价格，约为6.6元（比碳膜电阻高一些）。

    Relatively low cost: For example, a batch of 100 units of 1 kΩ resistors with ±1% tolerance and 1/4 W power rating costs approximately 6.6 CNY (slightly higher than carbon film resistors).

  • 温度系数较低：大致是50ppm/℃ ~ 100ppm/℃

    Low temperature coefficient: Typically around 50 ppm/℃ to 100 ppm/℃.

适用场景 Applicable Scenarios:

  • 对精度要求略高的精密设备，例如：（万用表的主板等）。

    Used in precision equipment requiring relatively high accuracy, such as multimeter mainboards.

备注 Notes：

  • 如何区分碳膜电阻与金属膜电阻？碳膜电阻通常为土黄色，⼀般采用四色环标记阻值。金属膜电阻：通常为天蓝色，⼀般采⽤五色环标记阻值。

    How to distinguish between carbon film and metal film resistors: Carbon film resistors are typically light brown and usually use four color bands to indicate resistance. Metal film resistors are typically sky blue and generally use five color bands for resistance marking.

贴片电阻 Surface-Mount Resistor (SMD Resistor)

结构图 Structural Diagram:

工艺 Manufacturing Process:

  • 厚膜：采⽤高温烧结的方式，把金属氧化物（氧化钌）和玻璃混合制成 的浆料，在高温下烧结在绝缘底座上。

    Thick Film: A paste made of metal oxides (such as ruthenium oxide) mixed with glass is sintered at high temperatures onto an insulating substrate.

  • 薄膜：采用真空沉积的精细工艺，把合金（镍铬合金）喷涂在绝缘底座上。

    Thin Film: A fine vacuum deposition process is used to coat an alloy (such as nickel-chromium) onto an insulating substrate.

特点 Features：

  • 厚膜 Thick Film（主要针对功率设计 primarily for power applications）

    • 厚度：通常在100微米左右。

      Thickness: Typically around 100 μm.

    • 精度：常见1% ~ 5%，最高0.1%。

      Precision: Commonly 1%–5%, up to 0.1%.

    • 功率：10mW ~ 10W。

      Power rating: 10 mW – 10 W.

    • 温度系数： 50ppm/℃ ~ 200ppm/℃。

      Temperature coefficient: 50 ppm/℃ – 200 ppm/℃.

    • 抗氧化能力相对好⼀些。

      Oxidation resistance: Relatively good.

  • 薄膜 Thin Film（主要为精度设计 primarily for precision applications）

    • 厚度：通常在0.1微米左右。

      Thickness: Typically around 0.1 μm.

    • 精度：常见精度为0.1% ~ 2%，最高可达0.01%。

      Precision: Commonly 0.1%–2%, up to 0.01%.

    • 功率：10mW ~ 3W。

      Power rating: 10 mW – 3 W.

    • 温度系数低：5ppm/℃ ~ 50ppm/℃。

      Low TCR: 5 ppm/℃ – 50 ppm/℃.

    • 抗氧化能力不是很好。

      Oxidation resistance: Not very strong.

适用场景 Applicable Scenarios:

  • 厚膜 Thick Film

    • 适用于：⼀般消费电子、汽⻋电子、家电等领域。 厚膜电阻覆盖了90%左右的消费类电⼦产品，应用十分广泛。

      Applicable to: General consumer electronics, automotive electronics, household appliances, etc. Thick film resistors cover about 90% of consumer electronic products and are widely used.

  • 薄膜 Thin Film

    • 适用于：⼀些精密电路（精密仪器、高端音响、测量设备等）

      Applicable to: Precision circuits, such as precision instruments, high-end audio, and measurement equipment.

图片 Image:

封装尺寸 Package size：

  • 2512，1210，0805，0603

  • 定义：长度和宽度，如0805指的是长度为0.08英寸，宽度为0.05英寸的贴片电阻

    Definition: The length and width of the resistor package. For example, 0805 refers to a surface-mount resistor with a length of 0.08 inches and a width of 0.05 inches.

备注 Notes：

  • 几乎不能从外观上去区分薄膜与厚膜。

    It is almost impossible to distinguish between thin film and thick film resistors based on appearance alone.

金属箔电阻 Metal Foil Resistor

结构图 Structural Diagram:

工艺 Manufacturing Process:

  • 把合金（镍铬合金等）与添加剂混合，做成厚度控制在几微米范围内的膜，涂在绝缘体上，随后刻蚀成不同的纹路来控制阻值。

    An alloy (such as nickel-chromium) is mixed with additives to form a film with a thickness of a few micrometers, which is then deposited onto an insulating substrate. The resistance value is controlled by etching the film into specific patterns.

特点 Features：

  • 精度极高：精度可达±0.001%

    Extremely high precision: Accuracy can reach ±0.001%.

  • 功率范围：1/16W ~ 15W 左右。

    Power range: Approximately 1/16 W – 15 W.

  • 成本很高：100个135Ω，精度为±0.01%，功率 600mW，需要5700元。

    Very high cost: For example, 100 units of 135 Ω resistors with ±0.01% tolerance and 600 mW power rating cost approximately 5,700 CNY.

  • 温度系数极低：约1ppm/℃，特殊制造的可达 0.05ppm/℃

    Extremely low temperature coefficient: Approximately 1 ppm/℃, with specially manufactured types reaching 0.05 ppm/℃.

  • 低噪声、耐静电、低感低容。

    Low noise, ESD-resistant, low inductance and low capacitance.

金属箔电阻是所有电阻中，精度和稳定性最好的电阻！

Metal foil resistors are the most precise and stable type of resistors among all.

适用场景 Applicable Scenarios:

  • 对可靠性要求极高的场景，例如：航空航天领域、军工领域、医学领域等

    Used in applications with extremely high reliability requirements, such as aerospace, military, and medical fields.
